# Software Engineering Viewpoints

This folder contains study material on different **Software Engineering Viewpoints** used in system design and analysis.

---

## Files Included

* **Technological Viewpoint**
  Explains system from technology side (tools, frameworks, infrastructure).

* **Business Viewpoint**
  Focus on business goals, stakeholders, and requirements.

* **Business + Application Viewpoint**
  Shows how business processes connect with software applications.

* **Combined Viewpoint**
  Integrated understanding of business, application, and technology layers.

---

## Purpose

These documents help understand how complex systems are analyzed from multiple perspectives, which is important in Software Engineering subjects and real-world projects like Healthcare Support System and Marketplace Management System.

---

## Usage

Open the PDFs to study each viewpoint separately and understand how they relate to system architecture and design.
